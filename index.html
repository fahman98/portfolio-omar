Who I Am

About Me

Dr. Omar Firdaus bin Mohd Said is a dedicated academic and administrator at Universiti Pendidikan Sultan Idris (UPSI). Specialising in Sports Management and Outdoor Education, he brings a wealth of knowledge and professional expertise to both the classroom and institutional leadership.

As the Director of UPSI's Alumni Centre, Dr. Omar Firdaus plays a pivotal role in fostering strong networks between the university and its graduates, strengthening community ties and academic reputation.
